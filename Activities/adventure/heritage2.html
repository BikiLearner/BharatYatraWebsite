Situated on the banks of the sacred Ganges River in the northern Indian state of Uttar Pradesh, Varanasi is a city that epitomizes the rich tapestry of India's cultural and spiritual heritage. Varanasi is a revered city for Hindus. It is believed to be one of the holiest places on earth and a gateway to liberation from the cycle of birth and death.
